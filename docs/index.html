Our Core Design Process

Set 1: Consultation

First we begin with a consultation session to determine what your needs and expectations are. Our Client are asked a series of question that will allow us to deliver precise and accurate results.

Set 2: Research ,Planning and Elegant Design

Our team of designers then begin to conceptualize and analyze your design needs and the key components identified during the consultation stage. The design team then start researching and planning innovative design solutions, crafting the perfect ambiance and character for your space.

Step 3: Refine and Reinforce

During this step we provide the client with a feedback report in which the space-layout, Design finish, Itemised costing and furniturching is discussed. We advise our client to schedule a consultation session so we can further refine and perfect our design solution. This stage terminates once the client has signed off and agreed to the designs, timelines, costings and finishes.

Step 4: Development and Manufacturing

Once the client is satisfied and has paid a deposit, manufacturing will commence. During this stage our project Manager ensure all activites occur within the specified time, to ensure we fulfill the agreed timeline.

Step 5: Installation and Completion

Once manufacturing has been completed the final stage consist of the installation and project completion.
